Privacy Policy — Parking Radar

Effective date: July 10, 2026

Parking Radar is developed and published by Ikshana Solutions LLC ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it's used.

Location data

When you tap Scan, Parking Radar asks for your device's location (precise or approximate, depending on what you allow) so it can find parking near you.

  • Location is used only to search for nearby parking and is not stored on any server we operate.
  • If you don't want to share device location, you can instead type an address into the search box — the app will look up that address instead of using GPS.
  • Your coordinates (or the address you typed) are sent to the following third-party, publicly-run services to perform the search:
    • Overpass API (OpenStreetMap data) — to find parking locations near the given coordinates
    • Nominatim (OpenStreetMap geocoding) — to convert a typed address into coordinates
    These are open community services operated by the OpenStreetMap Foundation and mirror operators, not by us. See their privacy policy.

Saved spots

Parking spots you save are stored only on your device (local app storage). We do not upload, back up, or have access to your saved spots. Uninstalling the app or clearing its storage removes this data permanently.

Directions

Tapping "Get Directions" or a rates/info link opens Google Maps or your device's browser with the destination coordinates. That interaction happens in Google's app/site, governed by Google's own privacy policy — we don't see or log that activity.

What we don't do

• No account or sign-in required

• No advertising or analytics SDKs

• No sale or sharing of personal data for marketing purposes

• No data collected from children; the app is not directed at children under 13

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
